Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field, at the centre
of which is a 22-yard pitch. It has traditional Test matches and newer formats like Twenty20. The
game's governing body is the International Cricket Council (ICC), with the Laws of Cricket
maintained by Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC) in London. Cricket is popular in South Asia, Australia,
New Zealand, the United Kingdom, Southern Africa, and the West Indies.
